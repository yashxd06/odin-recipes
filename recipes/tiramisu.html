Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone filling, and a dusting of rich cocoa powder. Light yet indulgent, it perfectly balances bold coffee flavors with smooth, sweet cream in every bite ‚òïüç∞

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Coffee liqueur (optional)

Steps:

  1. Brew strong coffee and let it cool.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar until creamy; mix in mascarpone and vanilla.
  3. Dip ladyfingers briefly in coffee.
  4. Layer biscuits and cream mixture in a dish.
  5. Repeat layers and chill.
  6. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
