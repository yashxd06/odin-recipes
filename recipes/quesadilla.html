Barbecue Chicken Quesadilla

Barbecue Chicken Quesadilla is a flavorful dish made with tender chicken tossed in smoky barbecue sauce, melted cheese, and toasted tortillas. Crispy on the outside and cheesy on the inside, itâ€™s a quick and satisfying meal perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Cheddar or mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Onion (thinly sliced)
  • Bell pepper (optional)
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Mix cooked chicken with barbecue sauce.
  2. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  3. Place a tortilla in the pan and add chicken and cheese.
  4. Top with another tortilla and cook until golden.
  5. Flip and cook until cheese melts.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
